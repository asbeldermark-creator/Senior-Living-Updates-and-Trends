About This Report

🎯 Who it's for

Operators, Directors of Nursing, care team leads, financial leaders, operations managers, REIT contacts, and marketing. Each trend card is tagged by audience.

📅 Cadence

Published on the 1st and 15th of each month. Trends carry continuity badges (🆕 New · 📈 Rising · 🔥 Still Hot · 📉 Cooling) across editions so returning readers spot what's changed.

🔥 Heat Score

1–3 flames reflecting recency and urgency. Three flames = breaking, actively covered by multiple sources in the last 30 days. One flame = building, worth watching.

📡 Signal Breadth

1–5 dots reflecting how many distinct source types are covering a trend: investment reports, trade press, clinical journals, operator surveys, and regulatory/legal sources.

🍽️ eMenuChoice Layer

Trends with dining relevance carry a 🍽️ badge. Each report includes a dedicated dining panel with eMenuChoice-specific content angles for dining and culinary teams.
